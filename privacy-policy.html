Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

Overview

iroLog ("the App") is developed by Kenta Torimoto. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

iroLog does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data — including photos and generated color palettes — is stored exclusively on your device.

Photos

The App accesses your device's camera and photo library solely to generate color palettes. Photos are processed entirely on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

Third-Party Services

iroLog does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Deleting the app will permanently remove all stored data.

Children's Privacy

iroLog does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
